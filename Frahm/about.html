    About

    Frahm & Associates CPAs is a professional services firm that specializes in accounting and bookkeeping for businesses and individuals.

    We have been under current management since 2002 and are located in the City of Spokane Valley. We have many clients on both sides of the Washington/Idaho border. The firm also services clients in several other states including: Oregon, Montana, California, Hawaii, Alaska, Arizona, Nevada, New Mexico, Utah, North Dakota, Minnesota, Nebraska, Kansas, Oklahoma, Iowa, Illinois, Ohio, Virginia, Maryland, Pennsylvania, Connecticut, and Massachusetts.

    Examples of Common Industries we service include: Appraisers, Attorneys, Construction, Financial Services, Landscaping Contractors, Insurance Agencies & Brokers, Hospitality, Medical Services, Massage Therapists, Mortgage Brokers, Property Management, Restaurants, and Trucking/Logistics Firms.

    When it comes to Accounting, Bookkeeping, Tax Preparation & Planning for businesses and individuals, the staff at Frahm & Associates CPAs has a combined experience of over 60 years. We put this experience to work for our clients every day; striving to help you grow through better management of your accounting and taxes.

    We know you are unique, and your needs are always our number one priority.

    For more information about how Frahm & Associates CPAs can assist with your accounting and taxation needs, Contact us today to schedule a complimentary consultation.

    Timothy Frahm

    CPA

    Tim Frahm started working in Public Accounting in 1990 after completing his BBA in Accounting and BS in Mathematics from Abilene Christian University in 1989. He holds a CPA license in Washington & Idaho along with having held a CPA license in Texas for the first 5 years of his career.

    Along the way Tim worked for three small CPA firms in Texas and Idaho as well as 5 years as a controller for a construction & service company. He has been associated with the Frahm & Associates CPAs firm since 2001 and as a partner of the firm since 2002.

    Tim’s extensive experience in small business has provided the opportunity to work with a wide range of companies including construction companies, real estate related firms, retailers and service industries.

    Time and time again, we have found that as we take the time to spend with clients, it’s the personal touch that makes the difference.

    Over the years he has worked with and volunteered for various non-profits including serving as officer on the boards of Silver Valley Habitat for Humanity and Family Promise of North Idaho.

    Tim has been married for over 20 years. He and his wife have four children, two of which are girls they adopted from Thailand. He resides in Coeur d’Alene, Idaho where he and his family enjoy the great outdoors, camping, hiking and backpacking. Tim also enjoys reading and sports, particularly basketball and volleyball.
